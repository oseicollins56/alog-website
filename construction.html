How a Project Runs

A Typical Project Timeline

Your build, demystified — here's what happens from day one.

Week 1 – 2

Feasibility & Planning

Site survey, cost estimation, architectural review and permit strategy.

Week 3 – 4

Approvals & Procurement

Permits filed, contracts signed, long-lead materials ordered.

Month 2 – 3

Groundworks & Foundation

Site clearance, excavation, reinforced concrete foundation pour.

Month 3 – 6

Structure & Shell

Frame, walls, roof, windows — weather-tight building shell.

Month 6 – 9

Services & Fit-Out

Electrical, plumbing, HVAC and interior fit-out coordinated with ALOG Interiors.

Month 9 – 10

Commissioning & Handover

Snag list, compliance certificates, warranty documentation and keys.
